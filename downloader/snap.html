SPOTLIGHT

View and save Snapchat Spotlight videos.

How to Save Spotlights

iPhone 📱

  1. Open the Spotlight
  2. Press and hold the video
  3. Tap "Save" or use Control Center > Screen Recording
  4. Go to Photos app to find your saved video

Android 🤖

  1. Open Snapchat app
  2. Go to Spotlight section
  3. Tap the download icon if available (varies by region)
  4. Or use screen recording: Swipe down > Screen Recorder > Start
  5. Video will be saved in Gallery

Windows/Mac 💻

  1. Use Snapchat web or BlueStacks (Android emulator)
  2. Use OBS or similar screen recording software
  3. Windows: Win+G for Xbox Game Bar
  4. Mac: Cmd+Shift+5 for screenshot/recording
